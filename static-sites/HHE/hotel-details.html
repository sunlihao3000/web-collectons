Charming Hotel 4* in the heart of Paris

Located in front of the Orsay Museum in a tiny quiet street, the Le Bellechasse hotel, charming hotel Symboles de France in Paris, offers you comfort and luxury of a Parisian hotel in a cozy, private and personalized atmosphere.This charming 5-star hotel, hotel Symboles de France in Ile de France, located in the posh 7th district of Paris, was thoroughly decorated by the famous couturier Christian Lacroix. This 5-star hotel embodies three principles: design, Parisian spirit and authenticity.The Le Bellechasse hotel, Symboles de France, is a charming hotel in Paris filled with quaint aura. It offers you discrete, warm and attentive service as well as refined decor, five star quality and personalized approach to your demands.Do not hesitate to chose the Le Bellechasse hotel for your next stay, charming hotel Symboles de France in Paris, at the heart of cultural life between the Louvre and Orsay museums, the Saint-Germain Boulevard and the Seine platform, the posh Saint-Germain-des-Près and the Esplanade of Invalid

Hotel Bellechasse: rooms

Each distinct with tailor-made decor, the work of Christian Lacroix, the rooms of the Le Bellechasse hotel are the acme of refinement and exception.The couturier Christian Lacroix took inspiration in the surroundings of the hotel « Couture » at the heart of Faubourg Saint-Germain. Diverse activities of elegant aristocracy depicted in Proust’s works, famous antiquarians (Louvre and Orsay Museums), official ministers, embassies, National Assembly unfolded in the background of design, music, theatres, galleries of modern and contemporary art.The Le Bellechasse hotel, 5-star hotel Symboles de France, is located in the 7th district in Paris and features 33 rooms. Each room is distinguished with an entirely personalized decor including: contemporary swanky design full of bright colors; truly Parisian style in copperplate hues blending copper, wood and antique patterns; moderate yet refined authentic decor.
